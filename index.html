Internal Hackathon

Our Internal Hackathon focused on building a deep learning powered Solar Flare Prediction system. The presentation gave an in-depth technical perspective covering solar science, dataset creation, ResNet architecture, and Explainable AI.

  • Solar Flares: Radiation bursts disrupting satellites, GPS, grids, aviation.
  • Dataset: SunPy solar images + CSV (flare/no-flare labels).
  • Model: ResNet CNN ‚Äì scalable, efficient for classification.
  • Explainability: Captum XAI with Saliency Maps to show flare regions.
